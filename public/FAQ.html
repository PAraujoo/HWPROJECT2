Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

Lunch is served Monday through Friday until 5PM.

What are your holiday hours?

We are closed on Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day to allow our members to spend time with their family and friends. Depending on the amount of business on the day before these holidays, the restaurants may close early.

What is the current promotion? Are there any specials?

To find out about promotions, specials, and news send us a form through the Contact page.

Do you offer group discounts?

Rather than offer a discount to one particular group of guests, we believe our responsibility is to make sure we're providing the best dining value possible to every guest who joins us for lunch or dinner.

Do you offer a rewards program?

We currently do not offer a rewards program, but you can sign up for more information through the Contact page.
